Systems from HEL

HEL Systems is an independent tech talk series focused on systems programming in the broader Helsinki area. The meetups are community-driven and not affiliated with any company. The talks are in English.

We are focused on high quality talks and expert speakers. Talks about engineering, not product pitches.

The topics of the talks focus around the following areas:

  • Operating systems
  • Compilers and virtual machines
  • Database and storage systems
  • Distributed systems
  • Networked system
